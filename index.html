My Travel Journal

These are my experiences of countries that i've visited

Japan

By far my favourite place of all time. They have beautiful sceneries, no pollution, futuristic, gorgeous streets, good food, and even more that you can name!

South Korea

Very similar culture and structure to Japan. The place is also filled with an aesthetic atmosphere mixed with a futuristic feeling and food that Melts perfectly in your mouth.

Malaysia

Everywhere I go in Malaysia, theres always beautiful skyscrappers everywhere. The food was good and there were a lot of fun places including Lego Land

Singapore

The neighboring country of Malaysia! This place has unique malls and trendy stores that contains popular items. Singapore is by far the easiest country to explore due to its small size!

Thailand

One of the most unique country ever! The food pallet is very unique that you can't find tastes similar in any other countries. A very free culture and a prideful country.

Hong Kong

The 2nd most free place in China. Astonishing Buildings with colorful lights and vibes. One of the best places to shop for merchandises of anything like games and shows. Cousins to Taiwan!

Italy

My top 3 favourite country. Has a nostalgic old-styled buildings and interesting relics left behind thousands of years ago even going back to the Roman Empire. Not to mention, they have the best food on this list.

Vatican City

The most holy catholic country since it's all about catholicism. You could see beautiful historic arts and relics when the doors to them are open. Very gorgeous country and technically Italy!

Switzerland

My closest favourite to Japan! The BEST scenery and feeling out of any country ive been to. The home to the best chocolate and cheese you'll ever taste in your life.

France

here lies the classic Eiffel Tower! The building structures are similar to Italy and has a very old and classic-feeling culture. Best breads you can find in the whole world!
